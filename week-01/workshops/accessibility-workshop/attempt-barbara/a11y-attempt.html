The drinks

Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 0.33 oz sugar syrup
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters

Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into ice-filled glass (preferably over block ice).

Suffragette

  • 1.5 oz Gin
  • 0.66 oz Dry vermouth
  • 0.33 oz St Germain

Stir all ingredients with ice and strain.

Emerson

  • 1.5 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Sweet vermouth
  • 0.5 oz Lime juice
  • 0.33 oz Maraschino liqueur

Shake all ingredients with ice and fine strain into chilled glass.

Old Friend

  • 1.5 oz Gin
  • 0.75 oz Grapefruit juice
  • 0.5 oz Campari
  • 00.5 oz St. Germain

Shake all ingredients with ice and fine strain into chilled glass.
